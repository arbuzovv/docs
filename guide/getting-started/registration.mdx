Registration on Ziplime is **free**. There is no cost to create an account and start describing your first strategy.

## Global Auth

Ziplime uses **Global Auth**, the shared authentication system used across Finam products. If you're already signed in to another Finam product, you don't need to sign in again when you open Ziplime — your session carries over automatically.

## Two independent environments

Ziplime runs as two separate environments, and registration is specific to each one:

- **Russian environment** — [ziplime.finam.ru](https://ziplime.finam.ru/)
- **US environment** — [ziplime.limex.com](http://ziplime.limex.com)

See [Two Environments: Russia & US](/guide/product/two-environments) for how the two differ.

<Note>
  The free tier has limits on the number of backtest runs and strategies you can keep. Higher tiers remove these limits and unlock more capable AI models. See [Pricing & Registration](/guide/plans/pricing-and-registration).
</Note>
